Designing a Web3 Enterprise Platform for Web2 Businesses Product Design

Mint is a platform that allows Web2 businesses to onboard their organization and their customers into Web3. The challenge was to design an enterprise platform that scales for organizations and their growing community while providing seamless entry into a space that’s mostly new for them and their audience.
Countering Sybil Attacks by Rewarding Digital Identity Creation Product Design

A Sybil Attack involves the creation of multiple fake identities to maliciously influence and gain advantage over a network. We designed Honeypot.money to incentivize the creation and authentication of digital identities to counter sybil attacks which can harm the blockchain ecosystem.

Designing a Web2.5 Checkout Product Design

At Mojito, I designed the white-label checkout plug-in we refer to as the ‘Payments Mixer’ which our clients can easily integrate into their own platforms. Designing a checkout flow that caters to both Web3 ‘noobs’ and ‘natives’ provides some interesting opportunities — how much could we tap into tried and tested Web2 patterns and what can be improved for Web3?

Account Management for Users New to Web3 Product Design

The Decentralized nature of Web3 makes it difficult for newcomers to navigate the space. I designed Mojito's Account Management section to allow both newbies and Web3 degens to easily navigate their account, view and interact with its contents.

Designing an Experimental Social Network with Quadratic Voting Product Design

Quadratic Trust is an experimental social network powered by quadratic voting to help build social capital. The number of votes a person receives is based on the quadratic formula where the cost of each consecutive vote from the same person increases quadratically, potentially giving more value to users who get a higher number of unique votes.
Integrating Web3 Collectibles into the Youtube Platform Prototyping

Web3 Collectibles can be a powerful addition to an existing Web2 platform's offerings. This unlocks blockchain information that popular platforms like Youtube can take advantage of to offer its creators new ways to engage with their community.

Memberships and Rewards Programs that Leverage the Blockchain Prototyping

Businesses can benefit from on-chain data to provide a better rewards program for their users. I prototyped how Mojito's core offerings can integrate into AMEX's existing membership and rewards platform, showcasing how POAPs and Collectibles can help with offering immutable, unlockable opportunities tied to live, tangible experiences.

How Interactive Sitemaps Help Audit a Growing Product Prototyping

Gitcoin.co continued to grow at an incredibly fast pace since inception and the current architecture of the site needed an assessment. I created an interactive sitemap for stakeholders to easily navigate the growing complexity of the product and its offerings to help determine which areas can be improved.

Status.im Visual Design

A collection of Visual Design work for the early version of status.im, a mobile Ethereum OS that gives its users the power to chat, and access a world of decentralized apps on the decentralized web.

The Blockchain Brief Visual Design

I was the sole Designer for the first two issues (November & December 2018) of The Blockchain Brief — from ideation to production, I was given free reign to design the layout and graphics for the publication.

OutClicks.net Visual Design

I worked with the VigLink team in San Francisco to design screens for their analytics product OutClicks.net. The project involved visualizing different kinds of crawler data, allowing users to easily spot monetization opportunities for their sites.

Dialpad Visual Design

I was a Visual Design Intern at Dialpad.com from 2015 - 2016. What initially was a summer internship turned into a year-long opportunity working with the design team to create assets for their rebrand from Switch Communications along with refactoring their UI assets from Adobe Illustrator to Sketch. I also coded high fidelity prototypes for their product features in HTML, CSS, jQuery.

Figma Rapid Prototyping Hacks at Gitcoin Prototyping

Gitcoin's Head of Design, Alisa March, needed help with creating rapid high fidelity prototypes to use for testing with users. I recorded a hacky way to create a prototype using transparent boxes on screens saved as images and show the team how different prototype interactions are possible with this method.

Dialpad Active Call Prototyping

Dialpad's Head of Design, Omar Lee, assigned a fun task for me to prototype an active-calling feature we were testing for Dialpad's native app. I created the high fidelity prototype in HTML, CSS, and jQuery as prototyping tools during the time weren't robust and readily available.

Flood Preparedness Kit Prototyping

The Flood Preparedness Kit is meant to be placed in public schools as well as other designated evacuation centers in the Philippines. It acts not only as an educational material, but also as a supplemental emergency kit whenever the need arises. It displays information made from strips of ductape on polytarp.
